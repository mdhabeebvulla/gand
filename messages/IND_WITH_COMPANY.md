For the state of {{Policy.PolicyState}}, Grievance and Appeal request must be sent in writing.

Advise the member to send their grievance and/or appeal request to **{{Policy.FinCompanyName}}**.

In the request the member must include their Identification Number, the reason for their complaint and their expected resolution to the complaint.
