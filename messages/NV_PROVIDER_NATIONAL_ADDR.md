In the state of {{Policy.PolicyState}}, Grievance and Appeal request must be sent in writing.

Advise the member to send their grievance and/or appeal request to:

**Anthem Grievance and Appeals**
P.O. Box 54159
Los Angeles, CA 90054

In the request the member must include their Identification Number, the reason for their complaint and their expected resolution to the complaint.
